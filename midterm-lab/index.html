About Us

From Vision To Installation We Power It All

With over 8+ years of experience, MFES Solar Energy has established itself as a trusted name in Pakistan's renewable energy sector. We specialize in delivering high-performance, customized solar power solutions for domestic, commercial, and industrial consumers.

To date, MFES has successfully completed 60MW+ projects, powering 800+ industrial facilities and 4,000+ residential installations nationwide. Our journey reflects not just numbers, but a strong commitment to building smarter, cleaner, and more efficient energy systems.

Our expert surveyors, engineers, and installers ensure every project is tailor-made – considering location, energy needs, and cost efficiency. From consultation and design to permitting and installation, we deliver solutions that transform the way energy is consumed.
